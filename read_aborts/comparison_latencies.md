# Overall abort benchmark

Measure the overall abort rates for SER and PSI.

Fixed parameters:

Sites=2
Ring=64
Vsn=500-250
Workload A (R=4 & R/W 2/2) or Workload D (R=3 & R/W 3/1) or Workload E (R=3 & R/W 3/3)
Latency=10ms
Preloaded
